Edinburgh

Winter might not be the best time to visit Edinburgh. The weather is dreary and you might find it too cold to explore the city on foot. However, Edinburgh is very quiet in winter (except in December). This means you can visit the city's top attractions and museums without having to deal with crowds.

Abderdeen

Enjoy an enchanting escape in Aberdeen and Aberdeenshire this winter as you visit the impressive Christmas markets and village in Aberdeen, find frozen fairy-tale castles set in their own winter wonderlands of Aberdeenshire, explore quaint old towns perfectly dusted in snow, admire impressive granite architecture, or discover beloved heritage museums.
